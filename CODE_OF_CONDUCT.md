# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

AURA is built with a reality-first engineering culture. Contributors are expected to be respectful, honest, and safety-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate clearly and professionally.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Keep disagreements focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and security boundaries.
- Avoid misleading claims about what the project can do.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, private logs, or user data.
- Encouraging unsafe automation.
- Presenting fake or placeholder behavior as complete.
- Ignoring safety rules to make a demo look stronger.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, restrict, or remove contributors who violate this code. Security and privacy violations may be handled immediately.
